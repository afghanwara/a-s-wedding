Our Story

Written By Destiny, Sealed By a Single Yes

Some love stories arrive with grand gestures. Ours arrived in the quiet certainty of one conversation.

In October 2025, our families were brought together in the most everyday of ways — two mothers, choosing fabrics at the same little clothing store, and a kind-hearted shopkeeper who, without quite realising it, wove the very first thread of our nikah. A simple introduction was made.

At the time, oceans lay between us — Afghan in Kuwait, Shifa in India. Yet on our very first video call, the distance seemed to melt away. Somewhere between hello and goodbye, Afghan simply knew — and he gave his word that very day.

By November 2025, with the blessings and duas of both our families, the rishta was joyfully fixed. And in January 2026, Afghan travelled to Kolkata, and we met at last — face to face — a moment we had already lived a hundred times in our hearts, before he returned to the UAE, carrying a piece of home with him.

Now, by the grace of Allah (SWT), we invite you to stand beside us as two families become one.
